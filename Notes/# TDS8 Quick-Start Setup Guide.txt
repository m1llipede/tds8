## 2Ô∏è‚É£ First-Time Wi-Fi Setup (Captive Portal)

On **first boot** (or after clearing settings), the XIAO will:

1. **Start** an open Wi-Fi AP called **`TDS8-Setup`**.  
2. **Run** a captive-portal on `192.168.4.1`.  

### Steps

- On your laptop/phone, **join** the Wi-Fi network **`TDS8-Setup`**.  
- **Open** your web browser to **http://192.168.4.1** (it should redirect automatically).  
- **Select** your home SSID from the dropdown, **enter** its password, and **Submit**.
